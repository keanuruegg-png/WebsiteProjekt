2. What personal data I process

This website collects only a minimum of data on its own:

  • Server log files: when you access the site, the host (GitHub Pages) automatically processes technical data such as your IP address, date and time, the page requested and your browser type.

No cookies are set and no analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) are used.

3. Sources of the data

The data either comes directly from you (when you contact me) or is generated automatically for technical reasons when the page is accessed (server log files).

4. How I use the data

I process this data exclusively in order to deliver the website securely and reliably, and to answer your enquiries and handle commissions. There is no automated decision-making and no profiling.

5. How I share data

I do not sell your data and do not pass it on for advertising purposes. Processing by third parties takes place only where it is technically necessary to operate the site (hosting, see section 6) or where I am legally obliged to do so.

7. Third-party websites and links

This website links to external platforms (Instagram, TikTok). As soon as you click such a link, the privacy policy of the respective provider applies; I have no influence over their data processing.

8. Children's data

This website is not specifically aimed at children, and no data from children is knowingly collected.

9. Security and retention

The website is delivered exclusively over an encrypted HTTPS connection. Data from contact requests is kept only as long as necessary to handle your request or as required by statutory retention periods.

10. International transfers

As the host (GitHub) is based in the USA, technical data may be transferred to the USA when the page is accessed. GitHub commits to appropriate safeguards (including standard contractual clauses).

11. Your rights

Under the Swiss Data Protection Act (revFADP) — and, where applicable, the EU GDPR — you have the right to information, correction, deletion, restriction of processing and data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact the address given above.

12. Complaints

13. Changes

14. Contact
